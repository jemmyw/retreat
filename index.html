Ruby Retreat!

27-30 March 2020 - Mt Cheeseman, New Zealand

What is a Ruby Retreat?
Ruby Retreat is an unconference run by and for the Ruby programming language community.
Picture yourself in the mountains with your smartest friends. The air is clear and so is your mind. There's no limit to what you can achieve here.
  • Scheming? Certainly.
  • Coding? It's likely.
  • Enlightenment? Maybe.
  • Drinking? No. Ruby Retreat is alcohol free.
What do I do at a Ruby Retreat?
This will be the very first Ruby Retreat, although it is the sequel to Rails Camp.
  • Work on Ruby projects with your friends.
  • Share your work with others.
  • Talk through gnarly problems with thoughtful, friendly folks.
  • Connect with the Ruby community.
  • Follow your dreams.
